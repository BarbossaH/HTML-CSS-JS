DPL TRADE MARK FILING SERVICES

Registering a trade mark is more convoluted than simply securing a domain name, or a company name; as the process takes (by law) a minimum of six months to effectuate. And, this is one reason why most business people elect to assign the task to an authorised agent.

Our up-front flat fees for filing an application(s) include all interim and ongoing communications concerning the following:

  • Preliminary brand/mark assessment/search
  • Filing the Application for Registration
  • Responding to Compliance Report(s)
  • Notices of Acceptance; Opposition; Registration
  • HOSTING of legally required TM contact address for the 10-year-term
  • First Cease & Desist Letter if, and when required, in the case of an infringement etc.,

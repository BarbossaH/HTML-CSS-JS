ABOUT US

Pacific Procurement Group (PPG) is a collective purchasing organization made up of independent residential land developers and builders. Because our members pool their purchasing power, PPG is able to negotiate hard cost reductions and volume incentives on quality construction materials from leading manufacturers and distributors. At the same time, PPG is able to provide meaningful value to our suppliers in return. PPG’s growing network of members and vendors continues to provide mutual benefit, helping each to maintain market-leading positions
HISTORY

PPG was founded by few premium land developers which has over 20 years experiences in land developing and building in New Zealand. As the delivering of better quality of homes become the goal of the land developers in later years, our founding firms came together to form a collective purchasing agreement that would allow them to compete with the volume buying power of the larger housing contractors. Pacific Procurement Group have added new members with residential construction operations since their formation. We have also expanded our list of suppliers and the products available through PPG. Our supplier contracts now cover as much as 90% of the materials required to build a home, all at a substantial cost savings. Our members continue to be market leaders, even through the recent housing downturn, thanks in part to the services offered through PPG.

MEMBERSHIP BENEFITS

Meaningful increase in discounts and rebates, improved service and substantial time savings are just a few of the benefits of being a member
HARD COST REDUCTIONS

PPG’s core focus is a reduction in the price per unit paid by our members for the materials that go into every house they build. Because they act together, our members have bargaining and purchasing power to rival even large national building companies. This collective influence allows us to negotiate unit and volume discounts from the leading suppliers in the construction industry

IMPROVED SERVICE

Service expectations such as warranty are negotiated at the group level and are strictly adhered to by our manufacturers regardless of the size of the individual member.

KNOWLEDGE SHARING

Because our members are evaluated on industry leadership, integrity, and altruism, PPG has also become a high-caliber professional network incorporating some of the best home builders and land developers in New Zealand. As a result, PPG is able to facilitate the sharing of best practices and new ideas across an open and cooperative group of company owners and world-class suppliers

TIME SAVING

Our members spend less time negotiating with their suppliers and have more time to spend growing their business

RAPID GROWTH

PPG has a direct line to the leadership of each of its member companies. Rather than submitting a proposal to each building contractor individually, potential suppliers only need to contact Pacific Procurement Group. PPG will vet the quality of the supplier and its building materials, and work through the process of negotiating a mutually beneficial agreement. This saves our suppliers valuable time and effort.

EXCLUSIVITY

Pacific Procurement Group works hard to sole-source as much of our building material and supplies as we can. This means that if you are a PPG supplier, your competitors are not. And this single sourcing agreement can be locked in for years, guaranteeing a lasting benefit for our suppliers, and creating strong incentives to work cooperatively to establish meaningful mutual advantages

PRODUCTS

Pacific Procurement Group offers specially negotiated discounts and incentives on 90% of the materials that go into building of homes. All of these construction materials, and the manufacturers and distributors who supply them, are carefully evaluated by PPG prior to becoming part of our program. These quality materials, provided by reliable distributors at below-market rates, help our members deliver superior homes while enhancing their bottom lines.
APPLIANCES

Thanks to PPG’s collective purchasing power, our member firms are able to purchase premier home appliance brands at well below market rates. PPG has done the work to negotiate the discounts that allow our independent home builders to bring industry-leading appliances into their building projects at a fraction of the cost

CABINETS, COUNTERTOPS AND HARDWARE

Pacific Procurement Group provides access to the latest styles in cabinetry, countertops, and hardware, allowing our members to create kitchen and bathroom designs that are sure to please any homeowner. Give your customers the freedom to select from among the best designs and highest quality options on the market without breaking your own construction budget

DOORS

Pacific Procurement Group has established discounted pricing agreements with suppliers of interior and exterior doors, garage doors, and sliding doors for your home construction projects. These top-quality doors are delivered to your job site by our fully vetted suppliers under a price-reduction and rebate agreement that you will be able to count on for years to come.

ELECTRICAL AND LIGHTING

Save money on the electrical and lighting component you purchase for your building projects with PPG. From electrical panels to the high-end lighting fixtures, PPG helps our members save on building costs while still providing their customers with the best materials available

FLOORING

Offering the best price on the latest styles and most durable materials, PPG has negotiated discounts with suppliers that cover all of your flooring needs.

HVAC

Thanks to our evaluation and negotiation process, our members are able to offer the best name brand, high-efficiency HVAC systems to their clients at below market rates.

INSULATION

Your home buyers are increasingly conscious of their new home’s energy efficiency. Provide them with some of the best insulation products available without raising your home prices or cutting your margins. Pacific Procurement Group has discounts available on all the insulation products our residential builders need. Reduce your customer’s energy bill and your material costs at the same time.

TIMBER

From floor joists to roof trusses, Pacific Procurement Group provides discounts to it’s members that cover all of their timber needs. Buying timber as a member of PPG means a fully vetted, reliable, on-time timber supplier.

PLUMBING SUPPLIES

PPG’s purchasing agreements covers all of the plumbing requirements for your residential building needs. From sewer line to PEX, PVC, and copper tubing, to water heaters and high-end faucets, PPG has what you need at a fraction of the standard cost.

ROOFING MATERIALS

Through Pacific Procurement Group our members have access to discounts on all the roofing products for their new home construction needs.

WINDOWS

As homeowners, your customers want high quality windows with style, durability, and energy efficiency. As a home builder you want windows that are delivered on time and available at a price that doesn’t cut into your profit margin. By purchasing with Pacific Procurement Group our members receive discounts and rebates from the best window manufacturers in the industry

PAINT

Pacific Procurement Group offers the highest quality interior and exterior paint in colors sure to please your home buyers. PPG provides access to special deals brokered with the best paint suppliers in the market.

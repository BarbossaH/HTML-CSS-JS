SmartVent ventilation system Kit4 Outlet

SmartVent Positive2 is a classic centralised positive pressure system that monitors temperature conditions in your home and at the incoming air source, to ensure that you are benefiting from improved air quality always circulating around your home. This proven SmartVent Positive technology has been used reliably in thousands of New Zealand homes over many years. Enter your preferred temperature into the easy to use, fully automated, controller and your system will ventilate your home, providing fresh clean air when you need it most.

As a positive pressure system, SmartVent Positive2 draws in fresher air from the roof cavity and fi lters it before distributing the clean air in, and around, your home via a network of ceiling diffusers as required. Air from the outside can also be sourced if the + Cool seasonal upgrade has been installed. Whichever the air source option, the air movement created forces the moist, stale air that causes condensation, mould and mildew, out of your home giving you better air for a healthier home.
